Council Grove Braves Baseball

This version focuses on cleaner baseball logic: fixed strike zone, clearer pitch timing, real base-state runners, a diamond HUD, and fielders who flow toward the ball or cover bases.

Batting

  • Use High/Mid/Low to protect the zone.
  • Tap Swing when the ball reaches the strike zone.
  • The strike zone is the white box behind the plate.

Defense

  • After the ball is fielded, throw buttons appear.
  • Pick the base with the best force/out chance.
  • Fielders now chase the ball, cover bases, and back up the play.

Pitching

  • When the opponent bats, choose Fastball, Curve, or Change.
  • Tap Pitch. Better timing and varied pitch logic creates balls, strikes, contact, and misses.
